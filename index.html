Frequently Asked Questions

~What are superfoods, and do they really exist?

"Superfoods" is a marketing term used to describe foods rich in nutrients and antioxidants. While these foods can be beneficial, there's no one magical food. A balanced diet of various nutritious foods is the key to good health.

~Is it better to eat small, frequent meals or three large meals a day?

It depends on individual preferences and needs. Some people may prefer smaller, more frequent meals, while others may do well with three larger meals. The key is to balance calorie intake and choose nutrient-dense foods.

~What are macronutrients and micronutrients?

Macronutrients are nutrients needed in large quantities, including carbohydrates, proteins, and fats. Micronutrients are required in smaller amounts and include vitamins and minerals.

~What is a balanced diet?

A balanced diet includes a variety of foods that provide the necessary nutrients, including carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, and minerals, in the right proportions to maintain overall health.
